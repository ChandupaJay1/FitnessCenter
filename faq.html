Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions below. If you still need help, feel free to reach out to us.

What services do you offer?

We offer a wide range of services including fitness classes, personal training, nutrition coaching, and more.

How can I register for a class?

You can register for a class by filling out our online form available in the registration section. Once completed, you will receive an email confirmation.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and online payment services such as PayPal.

Do you offer private training sessions?

Yes, we offer private one-on-one training sessions tailored to your individual needs and goals.
